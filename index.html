Piglet Run

Welcome to Piglet Run

    This is a multiplayer racing game with a predator and prey scenario
    In order for the prey to win, their goal is to make it to the end of the map to the door on the far left corner without being caught by the butcher
    The butcher's goal is to catch up to the prey before the prey has a chance to reach the end
